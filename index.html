Iyinoluwa Samuel Aboyeji

The Nigerian Serial tech Entrepreneur

Here is a timeline of some of Iyinoluwa Samuel Aboyeji's achievements till date:

  • 1991: Born in Nigeria
  • 2007: completed his senior secondary school at Loyola jesuit College
  • 2014: Co-founded Andela(A talent outsourcing company)
  • 2016: Co-founded Flutterwave(A payment processing company)
  • 2019: Founded Talent city Inc(a network of Charter Cities across Africa focused on providing the best quality of life for African technology talent)
  • 2019: Co-founded Moove Africa(a company providing accessible flexible car ownership for Africans)
  • 2019: Founded Future Afica(an innovation fund that provides capital, coaching and community to mission driven entrepreneurs)
  • 2020: Founded Partcloud(a company building distributed teams of African product specialists to enable best use of products and platforms for users over the world)
